The family trip is perfect for those looking to enjoy a fun and safe rafting experience with their loved ones. Suitable for all ages, this trip offers gentle rapids and beautiful scenery, making it an ideal choice for families seeking adventure together.

The big trip is designed for thrill-seekers and adventure enthusiasts who crave a more intense rafting experience. This trip features challenging rapids and exciting river sections, providing an adrenaline-pumping journey for those looking to push their limits.

The master trip is tailored for experienced rafters who want to take their skills to the next level. This trip includes the most challenging rapids and technical sections of the river, offering a demanding and exhilarating adventure for seasoned paddlers.
